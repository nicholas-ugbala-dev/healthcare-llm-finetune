* A healthy diet
* Avoiding smoking
* Limiting alcohol intake
* Getting enough sleep
* Reducing sodium intake

* For patients with mild hypertension, start with a low dose of a thiazide diuretic (such as hydrochlorothiazide) and increase the dose as needed.

* For patients with severe hypertension, start with a low dose of an ACE inhibitor (such as lisinopril) and increase the dose as needed.
